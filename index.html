Why FlagShip

Graduate hiring is broken. CVs look the same, university brand is over-weighted, and companies waste hundreds of hours screening low-signal applicants.

FlagShip exists to fix this. We run a selective intake process and curate a trusted early-career talent pool that companies can rely on with confidence.

For Graduates

FlagShip is for high-performing students and new graduates who want a stronger entry into the workforce. We assess based on university, degree, results, and overall potential. If accepted, you become part of a curated early-career network with access to top employers.

For Companies

FlagShip gives you direct access to a pre-filtered pool of top graduate talent across Australia. We do the upfront selection so you do not waste time screening hundreds of low-signal applications.

Company Access (Beta)

FlagShip is offered on a paid subscription basis. Pricing during the beta phase will be simple and founder-friendly.

Typical pricing will start from AUD $199–$399 per month, depending on access level and hiring volume.

How It Works

Graduates apply → We review and filter → Top talent is accepted into FlagShip → Companies subscribe to access and hire from the network
