FlagShip is where Australia’s most ambitious graduates are selected, showcased, and connected to the most competitive early-career roles across consulting, tech, finance, and high-growth startups.

What is FlagShip?

FlagShip is a selective network where top graduates are chosen based on academic excellence, experience, projects, commercial thinking, and technical ability. Those selected are showcased on the platform and exposed to the best graduate and early-career roles in the country.

For Graduates

FlagShip is built for students and new graduates who want to compete at the highest level. We select for performance, ambition, and real-world capability. If accepted, you gain access to elite opportunities and become part of a high-hunger peer network.
