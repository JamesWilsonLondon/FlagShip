What is Flag Ship?

Flag Ship is a selective network where top graduates are chosen based on academic performance, real world experience, commercial thinking, and technical ability. Those selected are showcased on the platform and exposed to the best graduate and early career roles in the country.

For Graduates

Flag Ship is for graduates who are aiming higher, those who want access to the most competitive roles and who back themselves to perform. We select for ambition, capability, and real world achievement. If accepted, you join a curated network of high performing peers and gain visibility with Australia’s top employers.
