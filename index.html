For graduates

Flag Ship is for students and recent grads who want to work in high ownership environments, ship real code, and accelerate their careers inside fast moving product teams. Entry is selective to keep the network high quality, but the application process is transparent and fair.

High signal roles only

You only see real product teams at startups and product led companies. No filler roles, no legacy tech traps.

Direct access to decision makers

Talk directly with founders and engineering leads. No recruiters. No CV black holes.

Career signal from day one

Being accepted into Flag Ship acts as a quality stamp at the very start of your career.

Selective, not intimidating

We review every application against clear criteria and typically accept a small percentage each cohort to keep standards high, without gatekeeping unfairly.
